Our History

THE TAU STORY

Established in 2024, Tau Logistics was founded by Koketso Thabitha, a dedicated girl who turned a challenging situation into an opportunity.

"After being retrenched from my job due to financial constraints, I took a leap of faith and started the business with a passion to provide for my family — as a hustling job."

— Koketso Thabitha, Founder

From those humble beginnings, Tau Logistics has grown to offer a range of industrial products including plastic drums, flushing chemical toilets, transport hire, plastic containers (20/25 litres), galvanized drums, and beddings.

The company currently operates primarily through WhatsApp for enquiries and orders, and this website marks an exciting new chapter — making it easier for customers to discover and engage with Tau Logistics online.

The name "Tau" means lion in Sesotho — representing strength, leadership, and the heart of a provider who refuses to give up.
