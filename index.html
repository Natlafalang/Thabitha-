Our Story

FROM HUSTLE TO TRUSTED BRAND

Founded in 2024 by Frans Chelane Masemola, Tau Logistics began as a personal mission — to provide for his family after being retrenched. What started as a hustle grew into a trusted supplier of industrial products across South Africa.

The name "Tau" means lion in Sesotho — a symbol of strength, courage, and community leadership. That spirit drives everything we do.
